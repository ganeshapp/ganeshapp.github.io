In November 2013 Tacloban, a city in Philippines was hit by Typhoon Haiyan.

I was part of a relief mission organized by Relief.sg. The relief team contained doctors and engineers. I was part of the logistic team and helped with the sourcing of tools, setting up of medical camp, clean water project and clearing out debris and rallying the locals to join us to clean up public areas.
