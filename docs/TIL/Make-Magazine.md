19 years of Make Magazine archive is avaialble online at [Archive.org](https://archive.org/details/make-magazine/). I remember in 2005 when I had just started college, I learned about Make Magainze. I got my dad to get me an online subscription and I would eagerly wait and implement the projects in the magazine. When I started the Robotics club in college, this magazine was one of the main inspirations.

But after college, I was more into coding than building physical stuff. So I didn't read Make Magazine for a long time. But now I plan to get back to [building physical stuff](https://www.gapp.in/blog/2024-11-24/) and I thought I should start reading Make Magazine again. Luckily most of the issues are available online and I can read them in my leisure.

I will start with the easier projects which involve Arduino and Raspberry Pi. At some point I will get a 3D printer and then I will try to build some of the projects involving 3D printing.
